Our Vision

We strive to give the user the best relevant information so that you have the information you need to make the best decision when applying to a college or planning a campus visit.

This is information from other users, who are experts in your school of interest. The ratings, posts, and information come from current students, alumni, faculty, or campus guests. We also have support services with applications for universities and financial aid from our team.

Why This Exists.

Arturo Lopez (Founder)

When I was applying to schools, I visited over 20 websites to check out the resources, the academics, the engineering program, the town, and more. School websites gave me outdated information that was also hard to find, and it always felt like I was visiting a site that said "we are the best school for anyone."

I created this platform, to give users the chance to get opinions from different parties. Asking questions and even forming a connection with people on this site will give college application process a different approach: one that benefits the applicant.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I have to do to become a publisher?

The contributors that are given access to post articles about a specific school will need to update profile and list the connection they have with the school(s).

Am I allowed to use emojis on this site?

You can use all the emojis you want to make your point, make sure you have fun with in and if someone likes your emojis, you get points.

When will the site launch?

We will be making an announcement about our public launch once we're ready. An anticipated date will be posted on here when we have one. Also subscribe to get up to date information.

Will all the features, including the blog and chat, be available at lauch day?

Yes, every service we described will be ready for users at lauch day.

Will there be a cost for using the platform?

There will be two user subscription options. The first will be completely free, and will give you access to everything but the professional advice from our team. You will get to see and post reviews, tips, and also get access to the chats.

The paid subscription will be for users who want to get help with resume advice, college application support, and financial aid application assisstance.
